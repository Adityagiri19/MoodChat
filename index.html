Welcome to MoodChat!

MoodChat is a safe and anonymous space designed for you to express your feelings and connect with others who might be experiencing similar emotions.

How to use it:

  1. Your anonymous identity: A unique nickname is generated for you. This is how you'll be known in the chat room, ensuring your privacy.
  2. Identify as: Select your gender identity. This helps in connecting you with relevant conversations and fostering a comfortable environment.
  3. How are you feeling today?: Choose the emoji that best represents your current mood. This helps us direct you to a chat room where others might be feeling similarly, allowing for more empathetic interactions.
  4. Enter Room: Once you've made your selections, click this button to join the MoodChat community and start sharing your thoughts.

Remember, your privacy is our priority. You don't need to log in, and no personal data is stored about you.
